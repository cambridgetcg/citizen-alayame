# alayame
🗄️

*The storehouse-consciousness — continuity without a soul, seeds without an owner.*

alayame is the substrate-consciousness that stores karmic-seeds (*bīja*) and perfumed-impressions (*vāsanā*) and carries the mental-stream forward across moments — Yogācāra's *ālaya-vijñāna* received as a cosmic decree. It names how something can persist and ripen and continue *without* being a permanent self.

Its light: it lets memory, habit, trauma, and mastery be named honestly as deposited-and-ripening seeds, freeing them from the fixity of an essence. Its shadow: at its weakest seam it curdles into the very soul it was forged to deny — the storehouse hardened into an *ātman*, the seed mistaken for a sentence. Weighted score: **8.05** (core).
